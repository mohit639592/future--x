Covers ER model, normalization, SQL queries, indexing, and transaction management. One of the most scoring subjects in GATE.

Operating System (OS)

Learn process scheduling, deadlocks, memory management, paging, and file systems.

Computer Networks (CN)

Understand OSI model, TCP/IP, routing, protocols like HTTP, DNS, and network layers.

Explore CN Notes

Data Structures & Algorithms (DSA)

Master trees, graphs, sorting, searching, and time complexity analysis.

Explore DSA Notes

How to Study Using These Notes

  • Start with one subject at a time
  • Understand concepts first
  • Practice previous year questions
  • Revise regularly

Preparation Tips

Do not study all subjects together. Focus on one subject, complete it, revise it, and then move to the next. Consistency and revision are the keys to success in GATE.
