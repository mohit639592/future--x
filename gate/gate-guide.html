GATE Exam Complete Guide

Everything you need to know about GATE – eligibility, preparation, marks, rank, and career opportunities.

What is GATE Exam?

GATE (Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering) is a national-level exam conducted by IITs and IISc. It is used for M.Tech admissions and PSU recruitment.

Who Can Apply?

  • B.Tech / BE students (3rd year, final year)
  • Graduated students
  • All engineering branches

GATE Exam Pattern

  • Total Marks: 100
  • General Aptitude: 15 Marks
  • Core Subjects: 85 Marks
  • Duration: 3 Hours
  • MCQ, MSQ, NAT Questions

Types of Questions

  • MCQ: Negative marking
  • MSQ: No negative marking
  • NAT: No negative marking

Negative Marking

  • 1 Mark → -0.33
  • 2 Mark → -0.66
